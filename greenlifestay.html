Accommodation Packages

1. Standard Wellness Package

3. Premium Wellness Retreat

Experience the ultimate wellness retreat with luxury accommodation, full access to all therapies, one-on-one sessions with therapists, nutrition planning, and guided nature walks. Best for long-term wellness and relaxation.

Why Choose GreenLife Stay?

Our accommodations combine comfort, nature, and wellness to create a serene environment. Each package is designed to support your physical, mental, and emotional well-being, making your stay at GreenLife truly restorative.
